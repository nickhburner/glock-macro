This page is not paired with a PC yet.

On the PC, open the A2 Macro Controller, enable the Remote panel and press Copy pairing link, then open that link on this device. The link carries the secret pairing key in the part after #, which never leaves your browser.
Status and commands are signed end to end with your pairing key. The relay only stores the latest status for a few minutes and can never control your PC.
